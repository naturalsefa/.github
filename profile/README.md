# Natural Sefa

Natural Sefa is a nature-driven wellness brand focused on herbal product development and modern digital systems.  
This organization contains the technical, research and development assets that power the Natural Sefa ecosystem.

---

## About the Organization

We work across two primary domains:

1. Herbal wellness products  
2. Digital product infrastructure and internal tools  

Our aim is to combine natural formulations with scalable technology, enabling reliable product development, quality control, commerce, and customer experience.
